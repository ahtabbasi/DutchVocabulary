# Dutch Verb Conjugation Practice - Regular Verbs (A2 Level)

Practice translating these English sentences to Dutch. Focus on regular verb conjugation in present tense, perfectum (past participle), and imperfectum (simple past). This set mixes questions, negatives, inversie, subordinate clauses, modal verbs, reflexive verbs, and separable verbs.

## Tips for Regular Verb Conjugation

- **Present tense:** Remove -en from the infinitive, add appropriate endings (-, -t, -en).
- **Perfectum:** Use "hebben" or "zijn" + past participle (ge- + verb stem + -d/-t); separable verbs take ge- between the prefix and stem.
- **Imperfectum:** Remove -en, add -de/-te endings based on the verb stem (â€™t kofschip rule).

Keep practicing these structures to get comfortable with questions, negatives, inversie, subordinate clauses, modal verbs, reflexive verbs, and separable verbs.
